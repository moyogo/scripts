UCAS script links

Updated 13 September, 2020

This page lists characters in the following Unicode blocks and gathers information about them from various sources.

  • Unified Canadian Aboriginal Syllabics

See also the companion document, Canadian Aboriginal Syllabics/Eastern Canadian Inuktitut, for a summary of the UCAS script and its use for Inuktitut.

Phonetic transcriptions on this page should be treated as an approximate guide, only. Many are more phonemic than phonetic, and there may be variations depending on the source of the transcription. In addition, different dialects of Inuktitut may show some divergences in pronunciation.
